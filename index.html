About Me - A Voice That Stands Out

With 15+ years of experience, I've voiced for over 55 multinational brands across radio ads, TV commercials, IVRs, corporate videos, audiobooks, and documentaries.

My voice is warm and versatile. Whether it is a radio ad, a TV commercial, an IVR, a corporate or training video, audio books, or documentaries, you will find the right voice right here. A voice that can change from a mother’s voice to that of a doctor, trainer, friend, your next-door-neighbour, or even a no-nonsense woman- that's how my voice can be described. Based in Dubai, I offer professional voiceover services in English, Hindi, and Urdu.

At age 10, a newspaper article about voiceover artists planted the seed for my career. After a long journey, I got my first voiceover job in 2009 and have loved every moment since.
